# Manas — Premium Mental Wellness App

**Build a fully interactive, premium mobile wellness app called "Manas"** (Sanskrit for "mind/soul"). The aesthetic must feel deeply luxurious and calming: dark midnight/deep navy base (#0D0F14, #12151C), soft lavender accents (#A78BFA), sage green highlights (#6EE7B7), warm gold details (#F59E0B), and frosted glassmorphism cards throughout. Smooth micro-animations on every interaction. Typography: elegant serif (Playfair Display) for headers, clean sans-serif (Inter) for body text. No harsh whites — use off-whites (#F1F0EB), misty grays, deep purples. Every screen must feel spa-like, intentional, and premium.

1. **"How are you feeling lately?"** — Mood scale with 5 icons ranging from stormy → cloudy → neutral → breezy → sunny. Lavender highlight.
2. **"What brings you to Manas?"** — Multi-select pill buttons: Sleep / Stress / Focus / Anxiety / Self-Growth / Creativity. Sage green highlight.
3. **"When do you usually practice wellness?"** — Three cards: Morning / Evening / Throughout the Day, each with an icon. Gold highlight.
4. **"How much experience do you have with meditation?"** — Beginner / Some / Experienced. Lavender highlight.
5. **"What's your name?"** — Text input. On submit, display a warm animated welcome: *"Welcome to Manas, [Name]."*

On completion → animate into the **Home Screen** (which becomes the default from this point forward).
