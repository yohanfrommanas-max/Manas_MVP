## 1. About Manas

---

Manas is a premium mental wellness app designed to help you build a meaningful daily practice around the things that matter most — your mind, your calm, and your sense of self.

The name Manas comes from Sanskrit, where it means mind or soul. It reflects our belief that mental wellbeing is not a destination but a living, breathing practice — one that grows with you over time.

We built Manas because we believe that the tools for mental fitness should be as considered and beautiful as the goals they help you reach. Too many wellness apps feel clinical, cluttered, or generic. Manas is different. Every screen, every sound, every word has been designed with intention.

**What Manas offers**

Manas brings together four pillars of mental wellness into one cohesive experience. Brain Training sharpens your cognitive fitness through games designed to build focus, memory, and mental agility. Calm Tools give you access to guided breathwork, sleep soundscapes, and curated music to help you find stillness whenever you need it. Journalling offers a private, reflective space to process your thoughts, track your mood, and grow your self-awareness over time. And across all of it, your Progress tells the story of your journey — your streaks, your patterns, your growth.

**Our philosophy**

We believe that small, consistent actions compound into meaningful change. A few minutes of breathwork. A journal entry before bed. A moment of stillness in a busy day. Manas is here to make those moments easier to find and easier to keep.

We are committed to building a product that respects your privacy, supports your wellbeing, and earns your trust every day.

**The team**

Manas is built by a small, dedicated team who are deeply passionate about mental wellness and the role that thoughtful technology can play in supporting it. We are always listening, always learning, and always working to make Manas better for you.

## 5. Help & Support

Present this as a FAQ-style screen with expandable accordion sections — each question is a tappable row that expands to reveal the answer, exactly like WhatsApp's FAQ screen. Section headers group the questions. Clean, readable, and navigable.

---

**Getting Started**

*How do I personalise my Manas experience?*
When you first open Manas, you are guided through a short wellness quiz that helps us understand your goals, your experience level, and your preferred time to practise. Your answers shape the content and recommendations you see throughout the app. You can retake the quiz at any time from your Profile tab.

*How do I track my progress?*
Your Progress tab is your personal dashboard. It shows your current streak, your mood history over time, your total wellness minutes logged, your Brain Training stats, and any achievements you have unlocked.

*What is the difference between the free plan and Manas Premium?*
The free plan gives you access to a curated selection of features across all four pillars. Manas Premium unlocks the full library — all Brain Training games, all Breathe techniques with voice narration, layered sleep soundscapes, Sleep Stories, offline music, binaural beats, AI journal reflections, and advanced progress insights.

**Brain Training**

*How do the Brain Training games work?*
Each game is designed to target a specific cognitive skill — memory, focus, processing speed, or logical reasoning. You can filter games by category, choose your difficulty level, and track your scores over time. Your best scores and play history are saved in your Progress tab.

*Why are some games locked?*
Games marked with a crown icon are available on Manas Premium. Tapping a locked game will show you what it involves and give you the option to unlock it.

**Breathe**

*What breathing techniques does Manas include?*
Manas includes five guided breathing techniques: Box Breathing, 4-7-8, Deep Calm, Energize, and Physiological Sigh. Each technique has a different rhythm and serves a different purpose — from stress relief to energy activation to rapid calm.

*Can I use the breathing sessions without sound?*
Yes. You can adjust or mute the ambient audio on any breathing session from within the session screen.

**Sleep**

*How do I set a sleep timer?*
On any sleep sound or story, tap the timer icon to set a duration. Options include 15 minutes, 30 minutes, 60 minutes, or until morning. The audio will fade out gently at the end of the selected duration.

*Can I mix sleep sounds together?*
Layered soundscapes — the ability to combine multiple sounds with individual volume controls — is a Manas Premium feature.

**Journal**

*Are my journal entries private?*
Yes. Your journal entries are private and encrypted. They are never read by our team or shared with any third party. You are the only person who can access your entries.

*Can I delete a journal entry?*
Yes. Open the entry and tap the trash icon in the top right corner. You will be asked to confirm before the entry is permanently deleted.

**Account & Billing**

*How do I manage my subscription?*
Go to Profile, then Account, then Manage Subscription. This will take you to your device's subscription settings where you can change or cancel your plan.

*How do I restore a purchase I already made?*
Go to Profile, then Account, then Restore Purchases. This will restore any active Premium subscription linked to your Apple or Google account.

*How do I delete my account?*
Go to Profile, scroll to the bottom, and tap Delete Account. You will be asked to confirm twice before your account and all associated data are permanently removed.
